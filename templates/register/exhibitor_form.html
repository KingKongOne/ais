Welcome to the complete registration!

The complete registration for THS Armada 2017 is open between 1st of July 00:01 and 4th of September 23:59

Make sure the form is completed and sent in before the last date of registration. It is possible to start with the form, save it by clicking "Save" in the right bottom corner, and then continue later. The questions in the form are divided into 9 pages and you can fill out the information in any order you like. Questions marked with (*) are mandatory. Some information in this form may be copied from your profile. Please check this information so that it is updated for THS Armada 2017. Your registration is completed when you click on the button "Submit" in the right bottom corner. When you have submitted the application you will receive a confirmation e­mail as soon as the application has been approved.

Please note that the complete registration is binding and you will be liable for all additional services chosen, provided that Armada is be able to supply your organisation with your choices.

BASE KIT (39 500, mandatory)

Included:
  • Stand area of 2 x 3 meters (Height < 2,31 meters)
  • Access to a Career Fair Host to help you build your stand and attract students
  • Full exhibitor information on website and iOS/Android applications
  • 2 tickets to the Armada Banquet
  • 8 Lunch tickets for the career fair days (4 per day)
  • Access to information desks, wardrobe and exhibitor lounges
  • Electricity and wifi provided at exhibitor stand
  • Logistics services for your equipment from delivery to the end of the career fair
  • Armada Transport, helps you transport your equipment from the fair
  • Climate compensation - in collaboration with Tricorona
Sustainability

THS Armada has been climate neutral since 2015. Climate compensation for your organization’s CO2-emission associated with the THS Armada fair 2017 as well as the organisation’s emissions during the year is included in the Base Kit. This year all climate compensation is done in collaboration with Tricorona.

The Banquet

Welcome to the Grand Banquet of THS Armada!

Dark suit November 21st Annexet, Globen

This is one of the biggest university banquets in Sweden where you and your organization will have the wonderful opportunity to network with many ambitious students in a casual atmosphere. The evening will start off with a refreshing drink and continue with a three course menu and spectacular entertainment.

You will be contacted in October to specify the names of the participants and any special dietary requirements. This information will be gathered before October 27th.

Tables

New for this year is that you also have the opportunity to purchase entire tables to the banquet. These tables will have 8 seats, a favorable placement and be branded with your company logo. You will have the unique chance to handpick top students of your choosing to this table, which will give you a great chance to deepen your connection with interesting top students that you have met at events, at the fair or to get to know new students that have shown great interest in your company.

Banquet ticket - Company representative

This year we have chosen to include 2 banquet tickets in the base kit for each participating company. Of course you also have the opportunity to purchase additional tickets if you’re so inclined.

Banquet ticket - student

Did you meet any interesting students during the first day of the fair? Let these students know more about your organisation and get to know them personally in a different setting by inviting them to the banquet. A ticket to the banquet can also be used as a very appreciated prize for students in a possible contest at your stand.

FURNITURE FROM STURE

THS Armada will continue the cooperation with Sture Exhibitions & Events AB regarding the construction of stands, rental of furniture and other career fair related services. Sture is one of the largest and most creative companies in Sweden within the field of expo marketing.

Aligned with THS Armada’s focus on environmental sustainability, we are very proud to collaborate with Sture that has received a gold rating from Svensk miljöbas due to their work to reduce their environmental impact. Sture are more than happy to help you with stand designs for both rental and purchase as well as graphic solutions (texts, signs, roll­ups etc.).

In a separate e­mail, you will receive Sture’s furniture catalogue put together exclusively for the Armada fair including three complete fair solutions. To facilitate the ordering process, just click the link to Sture’s dedicated e­mail address below and state your requests. For more information, visit Sture’s homepage: www.sture.se

For contact and ordering: gitte.friberg@sture.se
